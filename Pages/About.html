About Us

Kim Lee is a real estate professional specializing in the residential real estate market in Vancouver. With a deep understanding of the complexities of the local market, Kim has acquired vast knowledge and expertise in helping buyers and sellers navigate the real estate process.

Kim Lee offers a range of services including searching for homes,
buying a home, valuing a home, selling a home, and meeting with
her. She has a distinct approach and unique market insights, helping
her clients navigate the home-buying and selling process. Kim keeps
her finger on the pulse of the ever-changing housing market and
specializes in helping first-time home buyers. She has extensive
knowledge of first-time home buyer programs.

Kim Lee explores various areas in Greater Vancouver, including
West Vancouver, East Vancouver, New Westminster, Coquitlam,
White Rock, and Richmond. She also caters to clients interested in
luxury Vancouver real estate, offering the best deals on sought-after
homes. Vancouver is known for its opulence, and Kim helps her
clients find their dream homes in this beautiful city.

Combining a love for people with a passion for real estate, Kim
strives to provide exceptional service to her clients. She values
honesty, integrity, and determination in her work and is dedicated to
helping her clients achieve their real estate goals.

Whether you are looking to buy a home or sell your property in
Vancouver, Kim is here to assist you every step of the way. For
buyers, she will guide you through the home-buying process, educate
you on the current housing market, and negotiate on your behalf. For
sellers, Kim will help you net the most on your home, identify great
investment opportunities, and provide guidance throughout
the selling process.

Kim values building lasting relationships with her clients and is
committed to offering the highest level of service and dedication. To
learn more about Kim Lee and to view testimonials from satisfied
clients, visit her website at www.kimlee.ca. You can also contact her
directly at 778-997-8938 or hello@kimlee.ca.
